# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Regular employees and job-seekers in Saudi Arabia — not lawyers. Often reaching for
Haqqi at a stressful moment: just fired, wages withheld, about to sign a contract, or
unsure what they're owed. They use it at home, on a phone or laptop, anxious about money
and unfamiliar with legal language. They read and write Arabic.

## Product Purpose

An AI advocate that takes a plain-language description of an employment situation (or a
pasted contract), grounds the answer in the Saudi Labor Law via RAG, surfaces rights the
user didn't think to ask about, flags unlawful contract clauses, and hands them a concrete
plan to act — always citing the governing article. Success: the user moves from "confused
and worried" to "I know what I'm owed and what to do next," trusting the answer because
every claim is sourced.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, on-your-side. An expert advocate who speaks plainly, not a cold legal
database. Three words: reassuring, credible, clear. The interface should lower anxiety and
project authority without feeling corporate or intimidating.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-chatbot shells (bare ChatGPT-style stream, no sense of product).
- Cold fintech/legal navy-and-gold corporate look.
- The cream/beige "warm editorial" AI default.
- Cluttered government-portal density.

## Design Principles

- **Sources visible, always.** Citations and the case file are first-class UI, not
  footnotes — they're what separates an advocate from a chatbot. Show the work.
- **Lower the anxiety.** Calm color, generous spacing, clear hierarchy. The user is
  stressed; the screen should feel steady.
- **Arabic-first, genuinely.** RTL, an Arabic typeface tuned for screen reading, no
  bolted-on feel.
- **Trust through clarity, not decoration.** Polish serves legibility and confidence;
  nothing ornamental competes with the answer.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Arabic RTL throughout. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast; never light-gray-on-tint.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Legible base size (≥16px) for an anxious, non-expert reader.
